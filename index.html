This Kiribati to English dictionary is based on the Gilbertese-English Dictionary: Te Tekitinari n Taetae ni Kiribati ma Ingiriti, originally compiled in French by Father Ernest Sabatier, M.S.C., and translated into English by Sister Oliva. The original publication was released by the South Pacific Commission Publications Bureau in 1971. The source material was obtained from the National Library of Australia and has been adapted for this application.
